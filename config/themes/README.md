# Custom Themes Collection

## Underworld Themes (Dark)
- `underworld-red.yml` - Active - Blood red on pure black
- `underworld-green.yml` - Toxic green on pure black
- `underworld-blue.yml` - Electric blue on pure black
- `underworld-purple.yml` - Mystical purple on pure black
- `underworld-orange.yml` - Fiery orange on pure black
- `underworld-cyan.yml` - Cyan on pure black
- `underworld-pink.yml` - Neon pink on pure black

## Heaven Themes (Light)
- `heaven-red.yml` - Elegant red on white
- `heaven-green.yml` - Fresh green on white
- `heaven-blue.yml` - Serene blue on white
- `heaven-purple.yml` - Royal purple on white
- `heaven-orange.yml` - Warm orange on white
- `heaven-cyan.yml` - Calm cyan on white
- `heaven-pink.yml` - Soft pink on white

## How to switch themes

Copy the theme content from any file above and replace the `theme:` section in `../glance.yml`

Or restart with specific theme:
```bash
docker compose restart
```
